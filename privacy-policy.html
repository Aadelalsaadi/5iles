🎉 Good news: 5iles processes all files directly in your browser. Your files are NEVER uploaded to our servers. We cannot see, access, or store any files you process on our platform.

1 Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and encrypted password.

Payment Information: Payment processing is handled entirely by PayPal. We never see or store your credit card details.

Usage Data: We collect anonymised data about how you use 5iles, including which tools you use, pages visited, and time spent on the platform.

Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, and referring URLs.

  • We do NOT collect or store any files you upload
  • We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties
  • We do NOT use your data for advertising purposes

2 How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our file processing services
  • Manage your account and subscription
  • Process payments through PayPal
  • Send important service updates and security alerts
  • Analyse usage patterns to improve the platform
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

3 File Processing & Privacy

✅ All file processing happens locally in your browser. Your files never leave your device. We have zero access to your documents, images, or any other files you process on 5iles.

When you use tools like Compress PDF, Merge PDF, or Convert Image:

  • Files are processed entirely within your web browser
  • No file data is transmitted to our servers
  • Processed files are only available to you for download
  • Files are automatically cleared when you close the browser tab

4 Cookies & Tracking

We use the following cookies and tracking technologies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the site to function (login sessions, security)
  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our site (anonymised)
  • Payment cookies: PayPal cookies required for payment processing

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some functionality.

5 Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services:

  • PayPal — Payment processing (governed by PayPal's Privacy Policy)
  • Google Analytics — Website analytics (anonymised data)
  • Netlify — Website hosting and delivery
  • GitHub — Code storage and deployment

Each service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

6 Data Security

🔐 Security measures: All data transmitted to and from 5iles is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Account passwords are hashed using industry-standard bcrypt encryption and are never stored in plain text.

We implement security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Bcrypt password hashing
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls limiting who can access user data
  • Automatic session expiry

7 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification: Correct any inaccurate personal data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to how we process your data
  • Withdraw consent: Cancel your account at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@5iles.com

8 Children's Privacy

5iles is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at hello@5iles.com and we will delete it promptly.

9 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or by displaying a notice on our website. Your continued use of 5iles after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10 Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: hello@5iles.com
  • Website: 5iles.com
  • Response time: Within 48 hours
